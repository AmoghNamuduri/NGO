About Sri Sannidhi Foundation

Sri Sannidhi Foundation is a registered non-profit charitable trust established under the Maharashtra Public Trust Act, 1950, the Income Tax Act, 1961, and the Indian Trusts Act, 1882. For more than 15 years, our founding members have been actively involved in charitable activities and later came together to formally create this organization.

Our mission & Vision

We support underprivileged children and their families through initiatives in education, healthcare, nutrition, child protection, livelihood support, women's empowerment, and other social welfare Initiatives. Our primary focus is sponsoring the educational expenses of children in need.

  • 1. Sponsorship for school and college fees
  • 2. Direct fee payments to educational institutions
  • 3. Verification of academic and personal background
  • 4. Support for medical emergencies and social welfare cases

Our Initiatives

Sri Sannidhi Foundation supports a wide range of social initiatives. Below are some of our major program categories:

Education

Sponsoring tuition fees, school supplies, and supporting academically bright students from financially challenged backgrounds.

Healthcare

Supporting surgeries, hospital fees, and medical emergencies for children and families in distress.

Nutrition & Shelter

Providing nutritional support, shelter aid, and essential resources for vulnerable communities.

Child Protection

Ensuring safety, legal guidance, and welfare support for children facing hardships.

Impact Stories

Here are some real stories of lives transformed through the support of Sri Sannidhi Foundation:

From Loss to Leadership — Arthi's Journey to Becoming a Doctor

Arthi R., a bright and determined student from Bengaluru, reached out to Sri Sannidhi Foundation when her family was going through one of the toughest phases of their lives. She had lost her father to a sudden heart attack, and with no surviving relatives on her father’s side, her mother—who works as a tailor—was left to care for Arthi and her younger siblings studying in Grades 9 and 7. Despite these challenges, Arthi continued to excel in her studies. She shared her 11th and 12th grade marksheets along with her NEET certificate, reflecting her strong academic commitment and her dream of entering the medical field. Her sincerity, resilience, and passion stood out clearly. Moved by her dedication, Sri Sannidhi Foundation stepped forward to support her. Arthi secured admission into the BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery) program at DJ College of Dental Sciences & Research, Modinagar, Delhi. We committed to sponsoring her education for the full duration of the course, ensuring that financial constraints would never come in the way of her aspirations. Over the years, we supported Arthi consistently, paying her academic fees directly to the institution and staying connected with her progress. With each passing year, she grew not only academically but also personally, becoming more confident about her future. A Proud Achievement In November 2025, Arthi successfully completed her BDS degree—becoming a qualified dental doctor. Her achievement is a shining example of how determination, compassion, and timely support can transform a young student’s life. Today, Arthi is filled with hope and confidence. She dreams of supporting her mother and helping her younger siblings complete their education, ensuring they too build secure and independent futures. Her journey stands as a true reflection of the mission of Sri Sannidhi Foundation: to uplift deserving students and help them create brighter, stronger, and meaningful lives.

A New Beginning for Chandan — From Financial Struggles to Accenture

G. Lakshmi Chandan approached Sri Sannidhi Foundation with a simple but heartfelt request: support for his MSc Computer Science fees. With his mother working as a cook in a small company and being the sole earning member of the family, managing the education expenses for a two-year postgraduate program was beyond their means. Recognising his determination and the family’s situation, Sri Sannidhi Foundation stepped forward to assist him. We sponsored his MSc fees, ensuring that financial challenges did not interrupt his education or limit his future. Chandan worked hard, completed his final semester with dedication, and soon achieved a milestone that made all of us proud — he received a placement offer from Accenture. Seeing him secure a promising career and step confidently into the corporate world fills us with immense happiness. His journey is a beautiful reminder of what timely support and a student’s perseverance can achieve. Chandan’s success story is one more step toward our mission of helping capable students rise above financial barriers and build a brighter, dignified future.

More Success Stories on the Way

At Sri Sannidhi Foundation, these are just a few of the many lives we have touched. As they graduate and step into successful careers, we will continue to share their inspiring stories here. How you can help

Support a Child’s Education.

  • Your contribution can transform a student’s future. We directly pay school or college fees to the respective educational institutions—ensuring complete transparency and that every rupee is used for the student’s growth. By supporting a child, you help them continue their studies with dignity and confidence.

Our Modus Operandi

When a student or family approaches us for assistance, we follow a careful and transparent process:

  • - We collect basic information, including academic records and mark sheets.
  • - We verify the details through personal interviews and background checks.
  • - Based on our assessment and available resources, we sponsor the student’s education within our capacity.
  • - After sponsorship, we continue to track the child’s academic progress, offer timely counselling, and stay connected to provide moral support along with financial help.
  • - This ensures that the support reaches genuine beneficiaries and truly impacts their lives.
